About Founder

Sohail Ahmed Khan, Founder of UMIID International Association, is a distinguished author and senior lecturer based in Delhi’s Mukherjee Nagar. He serves as the Head of Department (IELTS, TOEFL, and GRE) at Wisdom Route U.S. and has significantly impacted the academic and professional journeys of numerous students across multiple institutes.

01 — Who We Are

Building India's
Future, Together

Every evening, we aim to transform talent and skills into true expertise — to benefit various sectors of our nation. Our vision is to make India a leading exporter in all domains by strengthening capabilities and promoting innovation.

We aspire to position India as a global leader in technology, textiles, and resources — contributing to economic growth and international competitiveness.

Our main focus is on education — ensuring every segment of society has access to quality learning. We introduce new policies and projects that empower individuals and make each city capable, self-reliant, and progressive.
